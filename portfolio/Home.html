Game Design

Making games is my main passion and focus. I put great pride into designing and developing fun and unique games that break boundaries. I have a wide range of skills, from programming to modelling to UI asset design. I am flexible and can work in most aspects of game development and am always willing to learn new skills.

Artwork

My art focuses on striking visuals and colors that pop. I can create illustrated game assets and concepts, but my abilities are not limited to that. My art is flexible and adaptable to your needs, weather it is a stand alone illustration or a series of assets for your project. I want to breath life into the art I create.
