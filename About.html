Dylan Simotes is an artist and programmer focused on interactive media and design. Dylan started drawing when he was only 7, and has spent the last 15 years improving his craft. When he was 11 he developed a love for video games that lead to an deep interest in how they are created. Currently studying game design at Illinois State University, Dylan is deeply passonate about developing all aspects of the game development process
